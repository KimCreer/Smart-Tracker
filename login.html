System Limitations

While Smart Attendance Tracking is an efficient tool, please note the following limitations:

  • Currently supports only Google Meet sessions. Other platforms like Zoom or Microsoft Teams are not compatible at this time.
  • Attendance tracking is based on the Google Meet API, so participants who join without logging in may not be recorded accurately.
  • Real-time tracking depends on the quality of the Google Meet connection and API availability, which may affect the accuracy in case of network issues.
  • Only registered users can access the system. Guest participation is not supported.
